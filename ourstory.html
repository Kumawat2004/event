About Bright Candle

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Bright Candle began with a heartfelt belief—that every celebration deserves to be crafted with emotion, elegance, and excellence. What started as a dream quickly grew into a full-fledged event design and planning company, known for its personalized experiences and impeccable execution.

With a presence in both Mumbai and Dubai, Bright Candle has curated countless unforgettable moments across destinations. At the center of it all is our founder, Bela Bhatt, a visionary with a passion for storytelling through events. With a strong background in creative styling and project management, she brings a rare balance of aesthetics and logistics—ensuring each celebration is as seamless as it is spectacular.

Our Vision

At Bright Candle, we believe that every event is more than a gathering—it’s a story waiting to be told. Our vision is to bring those stories to life with intention, creativity, and a touch of sparkle. We immerse ourselves in our clients’ journeys, understanding their dreams and values to transform ideas into unforgettable memories.
